FACTS ABOUT ST BERNARD

Saint Bernards are known to big curly loving dogs, gentle and tolerant in spite of their size. They are family dogs and loved by everyone. Saints are naturally friendly, intelligent and are not easily made aggressive unless it relates to its owners or family members. One important thing to note about this breed is that they are prone to health conditions so to have this dog one should be ready to take care of it and keep it in a clean environment.

SIZE:Saint Bernards vary in weight and Heights. For the male breeds, they weigh as much 140-180 pounds and can grow as large as 27in while for the female breeds they weigh between 120-140 pounds and are slightly smaller than the male breeds as they can only grow to 25in.

MAJOR CHARCTERSITICS:The major characteristic of Saint Bernard is that they are an all-round friendly dog with really droopy eyes, floppy ears and have two coat types: they can either be shorthaired and longhaired meaning they can be very fluffy. They are friendly with both children and adults, friendly toward strangers and also towards other dogs.

EXERCISE:Generally, Saint Bernards require only little amounts of exercise as they can gte fatigued easily, and majorly reduce taking them out in the heat because they can have heat strokes and heat exhaustion.

TRAINING:Without proper training, Saint Bernards can wreak havoc in your apartments and this is a result of their size. Here at puppy love we also help to train dogs to make them more comfortable in homes and so they can be excellent companions to have in the home.

FEEDING: The way a dog eats depends on its size, activities carried outage and other factors. Saint Bernards because of their size require a lot of food. However one should be careful when feeding Saint Bernards as they like to eat and can become obese.

Life-Span:8-10years
