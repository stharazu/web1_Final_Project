FACTS ABOUT HUSKY

Huskies are very active and hardworking dogs, the Siberian Huskies are in the same class of dogs as splitz. Huskies have beautiful icy eyes or sometimes brown eyes and in rare cases one brown eye and one blue eye. Huskies have their origin from Siberia where they were used mostly for pulling sleds.

SIZE:Male huskies weigh about 35-60 pounds, while female huskies weigh in at 35-60 pounds. Male Huskies have their height at 54-60cm while female huskies 50-56cm.

MAJOR CHARCTERSITICS:Huskies are often mistaken to look like wolves because of their similar characteristics. When talking about dogs huskies are of the oldest breeds. It has its standing erect ears, thick coats, more than other breeds of dogs which protects it from very cold weathers. They are of different colors white, black, black and white, silver brown, etc. Huskies are generally fun dogs and can be attached to their families in no time. They are very intelligent dogs and as such training them will not be difficult.

EXERCISE:Huskies love to exercise, nut the exercise should vary based on weather conditions. For those in cold weather should take their huskies for skies, but where is temperature is hot Huskies should not be over exercised as it can lead to overheating.

Life-Span:11-13years
