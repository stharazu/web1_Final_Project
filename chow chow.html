FACTS ABOUT CHOW CHOW

Chow Chow is not popularly known to be gregarious breed they tend to be independent and not outgoing dogs. They can be aggressive to strangers but very protective of their family. They can so be overweight when overfed.

SIZE:Chow Chow vary in weight and Heights. For the male breeds, they weigh as much 45-70 pounds and can grow between 48-56cm while for the female breeds they weigh between 45-70 pounds and are slightly smaller than the male breeds as they can only grow between 46-61cm.

MAJOR CHARCTERSITICS:Chow Chow dogs are popularly known for their blue-black tongues, they are strong and have a lot of furs. Their furs are of two coats which is the rough coat or long coat and the short coats. Chow Chow are dogs that enjoy cold weather and do not like hot weather, they are dogs that averagely can adapt to situations. They are a little bit difficult to train and have a very large potential for mouthiness as they like to chew a lot of things that come their way. Generally, chow chow are not sociable dogs either to man or dogs.

EXERCISE: Chow Chow is expected to have at least 20minutes work to keep them in good shape.

Life-Span:12-15years
