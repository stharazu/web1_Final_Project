FACTS ABOUT Poodle

Trying to win a dog competition? Poodles are certainly the best for the job. Behind its elegance and beauty is a dog that is very loving and fun to have as they are people friendly. Poddles are of three types from “the toy, to the miniature and the standard”.

SIZE:The toy poodle has it height at 10inches and weighs about 6-9 nine pounds, while the miniature poodle has its height at 11-15 inches weighing about 15-17 pounds and the Standard poodle which is usually 22 inches weighs about 45-70 pounds and the females weigh 45-60pounds.

MAJOR CHARCTERSITICS: Poodles can live comfortably in homes, they are very good dogs for people have not had dogs before. They are friendly with both children and adults, friendly toward strangers and also towards other dogs. poodles are very easy to train and are very intelligent dogs too. Just like most gods poodles also have the tendency of mouthiness and can chew on anything within their reach. They also can drool a lot.
