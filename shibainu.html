FACTS ABOUT SHIBA

Shiba Inu dog has a lot to its cute features, it's an interesting breed. Shiba has its origin from Japan and also part of the spitz class of dogs. Shiba was originally a hunting dog for game before it became a pet dog.

SIZE:The male stands at 35-43cm while the female stands at 33-41cm, and they weigh 8-11kg, and 6.8-9kg accordingly.

MAJOR CHARCTERSITICS: Shiba's have different colour coats including white, red, cream, etc. Shiba’s three major personalities are tied to three Japanese words “Kan-i, Ryosei, and Soboku.” The first meaning that Shiba are fearless dogs, the second describes how gentle the Shiba can be despite its fearless nature and finally describes as Shiba is a combination of both fearless, happy and impulsive in its behaviour.

EXERCISE:The exercise for Shiba should not be too intense as they can become weak with too much exercise.

Life-Span:12-15years
